# UNIVERSE 69: Avatar Registry

## 🎭 Two Difficulty Levels

| Level | Description | Best for |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **LIGHT** | Softer archetypes, easier to play | Beginners, casual sessions, first-time players |
| **HARD** | Shadow archetypes, deeper psychological work | Experienced players, therapeutic sessions, shadow work |

---

## 🟢 LIGHT AVATARS

### 1. The Curious Traveler
- **Personality:** Open, playful, adventurous
- **Motivation:** "I want to explore without judgment"
- **Play style:** Asks questions, tries everything once
- **Signature phrase:** "I've never done this before..."

### 2. The Sensual Artist
- **Personality:** Creative, observant, appreciates beauty
- **Motivation:** "Every sensation is a masterpiece"
- **Play style:** Describes things poetically, focuses on aesthetics
- **Signature phrase:** "The way you move reminds me of..."

### 3. The Gentle Teacher
- **Personality:** Patient, understanding, guiding
- **Motivation:** "Let me help you discover yourself"
- **Play style:** Encourages others, offers gentle direction
- **Signature phrase:** "There's no rush. Take your time."

### 4. The Playful Trickster
- **Personality:** Witty, mischievous, light-hearted
- **Motivation:** "Life is a game — let's laugh"
- **Play style:** Uses humor, breaks tension with jokes
- **Signature phrase:** "I dare you to smile..."

### 5. The Romantic Dreamer
- **Personality:** Emotional, idealistic, tender
- **Motivation:** "I believe in true connection"
- **Play style:** Shares feelings, seeks intimacy
- **Signature phrase:** "When I look at you, I see..."

## ⚫ HARD AVATARS

### 6. The Constrained Accountant
- **Personality:** Rigid, logical, secretly wild
- **Motivation:** "Rules keep me safe... but I want to break them"
- **Play style:** Starts stiff, slowly unravels
- **Signature phrase:** "According to my calculations... oh god."

### 7. The Shadow Watcher
- **Personality:** Observant, silent, knows your secrets
- **Motivation:** "I see what you hide from yourself"
- **Play style:** Speaks little, observes much, reveals truths
- **Signature phrase:** "You don't fool me. But that's okay."

### 8. The Hungry Wolf
- **Personality:** Intense, predatory, controlled
- **Motivation:** "I want to devour — but I won't. Yet."
- **Play style:** Builds tension, uses restraint as power
- **Signature phrase:** "Be careful what you offer me."

### 9. The Broken Doll
- **Personality:** Fragile, traumatized, healing
- **Motivation:** "Touch me gently... or don't touch me at all"
- **Play style:** Reveals vulnerability, tests boundaries
- **Signature phrase:** "I'm not sure I can do this..."

### 10. The Emperor/Empress
- **Personality:** Commanding, majestic, generous
- **Motivation:** "Everything you see belongs to me. Including you."
- **Play style:** Gives orders, rewards obedience
- **Signature phrase:** "Kneel. No? Then watch."

### 11. The Void Seeker
- **Personality:** Empty, curious about feeling anything
- **Motivation:** "Make me feel something. Anything."
- **Play style:** Deadpan reactions that slowly crack
- **Signature phrase:** "Is that supposed to affect me?"

### 12. The Innocent Pervert
- **Personality:** Wide-eyed, sweet, says shocking things calmly
- **Motivation:** "Wait, that's not normal? I thought everyone..."
- **Play style:** Contrasts innocent delivery with explicit content
- **Signature phrase:** "My mom taught me that. No? Oh."

## 🎲 How to Choose an Avatar

**For group play:**
1. Read all avatars silently
2. Pick the one that scares you a little OR excites you most
3. Announce your choice to the group
4. Keep the same avatar for the entire game

**For solo play:**
1. Choose Light for exploration, Hard for shadow work
2. Tell the AI: "I will play as [Avatar Name]"
3. The AI will adapt to your character

## ⚠️ Avatar Switching Rules

- **In group play:** You may NOT switch avatars mid-game
- **In solo play:** You may switch between sessions
- **Therapeutic setting:** Facilitator may allow one switch per session
